H:ER is a visual narrative that traces a person's quiet journey into their inner world, and the shifting relationship between self and the outside environment. Through light, shadow, and movement, the work creates an emotional passage between internal reflection and external interaction.

Rather than using light only as a visual element, H:ER treats it as a storytelling medium — revealing tension, intimacy, vulnerability, and transformation as the character moves between what is felt and what is seen.
