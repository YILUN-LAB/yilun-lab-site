The piece is a quick-fire self-portrait: pure speed, raw power, and light fused into one relentless heartbeat. Each razor-sharp step detonates a strobe of light, like sugar crystals bursting in mid-air. Pulses of white and electric blue chase his rapid turns, freezing silhouettes before they shatter into motion again.

**Dancer:** Seven · **Music:** Boba
