THROUGH LIMITS portrays the experience of being trapped within a self-made loop — a state of repetition, hesitation, and invisible confinement. The work follows a process of struggle and release, tracing how a person confronts inner limits, breaks through cycles of self-restraint, and gradually finds a new sense of direction.

Through the shifting use of light, shadow, and spatial rhythm, the piece transforms an internal psychological journey into a visible narrative of tension, awakening, and renewal.
